YYYMMDD= date
HH=  hour
DD= Wind direction(degrees)
FH= Hourly average wind(in 0.1 m/s)
FF =  Wind speed  last 10 minuts(in 0.1 m/s)
FX= Highest wind gust(in 0.1m/s) last hour
T = temperature( in 0.1 degrees) 1.5m h
T10n = Minimum temperature (in 0.1 degrees Celsius) at 10 cm h
TD = Dew point temperature (in 0.1 degrees Celsius) at 1.50 m h
Q = Global radiation (in J/cm2) per hour
DR = Precipitation duration (in 0.1 hour) hourly division
RH = Hourly precipitation amount (in 0.1 mm)
P = Air pressure (in 0.1 hPa)
VV = air transparaty (0=less than 100m, 1=100-200m, 2=200-300m,..., 49=4900-5000m, 50=5-6km, 56=6-7km, 57= 7-8km, ..., 79=29-30km, 80=30-35km, 81=35-40km,..., 89=more than 70km)/Air transparency (in 100m)
N= Cloud cover (coverage of the upper sky in eighths)(9=upper sky invisible)
U = Relative humidity (in percent) at 1.50 m h
WW = Weather code (00-99)
IX = Weather code indicator for the method of observation at a manned or automatic station (1=manned using code from visual observations, 2.3=manned and omitted (no significant weather phenomenon, no data), 4=automatic and recorded (using code from visual observations), 5.6=automatic and omitted (no important weather phenomenon, no data), 7=automatic using code from automatic observations) / Indicator present weather code (1=manned and recorded (using code from visual observations) , 2.3=manned and omitted (no significant weather phenomenon to report, not available), 4=automatically recorded (using code from visual observations), 5.6=automatically omitted (no significant weather phenomenon to report, not available), 7=automatically set (using code from automated observations)
R = Rain (0=did not occur, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or during the observation / Rainfall 0=no occurrence, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or at the time of observation)
S = Snow (0=did not occur, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or during the observation / Snow 0=no occurrence, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or at the time of observation)
O = Thunder (0=did not occur, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or during the observation / Thunder 0=no occurrence, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or at the time of observation)
Y = Ice formation (0=did not occur, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or during the observation / Ice formation 0=no occurrence, 1=occurred during the preceding hour and/or at the time of observation)
